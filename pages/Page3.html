Beauty for Ashes

September 08 2021

Dear, The long silence and the cold war raging on the inside of you. The pain and the heavy heart, I am not a stranger to your every fear, your teardrops, I see, for everytime your heart skipped a bit and appeared to the falling apart I recognize, all the feelings of being left at sea, stranded with no help in sight. I understand, for I am a man familiar with sorrow. You ask why you have to bear this pain or suffering why it ever came your way? Why it had to be you. You see, I felt the same thing on the cross, it is why I know pain, suffering, tribulation, every test. All of these were borne on me, excruciating pain, but I did bear them, so you don't have to. Listen, being nailed on the Cross, going into the grave and defeating death is all the Hope you need today. Your leverage is sure, because on the cross it was all finished - It's all over, you won. As you go through the throngs of fire, I am with you. Anxiety, cannot stand the sight of my love For you You are loudly loved. “Don’t let your hearts be troubled. Trust in God, and trust also in me. John 14:1 This High Priest of ours understands our weaknesses, for he faced all of the same testings we do, yet he did not sin. Hebrews 4:15 With love, Jesus.
